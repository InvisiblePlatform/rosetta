Privacy policy

We do not track you. We try our best to minimise any data we gather. We use self-hosted free and open tools whenever possible and we do not share your data with third parties.

Privacy is a fundamental human right that your other rights depend on. We exist to create technology that protects your human rights. So, in a nutshell, our privacy policy is that we exist to protect your privacy.

Video

Our video is hosted on YouTube. We do use YouTube analytics in our video player.

PayPAL

We do not store any of your payment-related information, including your email address. The data held by PayPal to process your donations and patronages is covered by PayPal.
